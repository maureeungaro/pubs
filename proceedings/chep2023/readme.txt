Dear Maurizio,

You are registered as a speaker of

GEMC: a database driven Monte Carlo simulation program (Thursday 11 May 2023, 11:30 - 11:45)
 at CHEP 2023 conference. We invite all authors to submit proceedings papers to accompany their plenary, parallel, or poster CHEP 2023 presentations.

The CHEP 2023 proceedings will be published in the EPJ Web of Conferences.
The deadline for the initial submission of your articles is: Fri Sep 8 at 23:59 UTC

- Each article shall have at most 8 pages (references included) and use single-column format. This applies to parallel oral presentations and posters.
- Plenary oral presentation articles shall have at most 12 pages.
- Each article must be submitted by a single person who shall represent all co-authors of the article.
- Articles must be submitted in PDF form. Please use the given LaTeX or Microsoft Word template, but only submit the PDF version in the end.
  Please check that the PDF version meets the provided PDF guidelines.
- Please follow the clearly documented style guidelines. Adherence to those guidelines will be checked by the reviewers and violations
  will have to be corrected by the authors.
- Please provide proper references to software packages and frameworks that were vital for the work covered by your article.
  Suggested guidelines are available from Matthew Feickert talk at CHEP2023 (presentation)
- Please print the provided copyright form, fill it out and scan the completed form.
  Alternatively, use a PDF editor instead, but mind that an actual signature is required.
  Please upload the completed form along with your articles, as explained below.
- Mind that you may need to have your article approved first by your experiment and/or institute: please check what applies in your case!

To upload your contributions to the proceedings please log in to CHEP 2023 Indico, then navigate to Submission to Proceedings,
select the relevant contribution, and submit using "Submit Paper".

Please contact chep2023-pc-chairs AT cern.ch for any questions or comments about the publication procedures.

Best regards,
